What Factors Determine the Popularity of Songs on Spotify (2022)

DS 4200, Group 17: Fiona Chen, Fin Li, Owen Hong

Topic Motivation

Understanding what makes a song popular is useful information for artists, producers, and others in the music industry, with widely-used music streaming platforms being a reliable source of data on how listeners engage with songs. Using Spotify data from 2022, this project explores how song characteristics—such as duration, key, energy, and genre—correlate with a song's popularity on Spotify. By analyzing these factors, we aim to uncover trends that influence a song's popularity, offering insights into listener preferences and music consumption.
